> # 윈도우10에 workbench를 설치해보자.
> 시작메뉴에 MySQL Installer - Community를 클릭한다.
> ![workbench](/assets/images/wb0.JPG)
> <br>
> <br>
> <br>
> Add 클릭
> ![workbench](/assets/images/wb1.JPG)

> <br>
> <br>

> ![workbench](/assets/images/wb2.JPG)
> <br>
> <br>
> <br>
> Execute 클릭
> ![workbench](/assets/images/wb3.JPG)
> <br>
> <br>
> <br>
> Next 클릭

> <br>
